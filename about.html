About the project

Why we built StudentHub+

A student's day is scattered across a dozen apps — a notes app for tasks, a spreadsheet for GPA, a random timer app for focus sessions. StudentHub+ brings the essentials into one dashboard, built entirely with vanilla web technologies for a hackathon.

Our Mission

Give students one clean, fast, distraction-free place to manage academics — no sign-up, no clutter, no ads.

Tech Stack

Built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build tools. Runs entirely in your browser.

Your Data, Your Browser

All your tasks, assignments, and schedules are saved locally via LocalStorage — nothing is sent to a server.
